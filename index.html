Partutti

PDF tools for brass band musicians

PDF Splitter

Split brass band sheet music PDFs by instrument. Automatically detects instrument names and creates individual parts.

PDF Page Combiner

Combine 2 pages into 1 for A5 printing. Process multiple PDFs at once with flexible pairing options.

PDF Merger

Merge one common PDF (e.g., lyrics) with all instrument parts. Adds the common pages to the beginning of each part.

PDF Assembler

Combine all parts into one master PDF with configurable replicas. Set how many copies of each part you need (e.g., 2 euphoniums).
